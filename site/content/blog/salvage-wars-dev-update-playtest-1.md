***Salvage Wars*** is a single-player extraction real-time tactics game where you command a salvage crew picking through battlefields beneath an ongoing orbital war.

This build marks **Playtest 1**, the very first playable milestone. 

The **primary goal** of this initial playtest is to evaluate the **combat mechanics and their potential to create engaging and diverse tactical situations.** While basic overworld screens are present, critical gameplay loops like selling loot or unit upgrades are not yet implemented. 

### What's In This Build

- **Single Battle Map:** A focused combat scenario with non-randomized enemy spawns to test basic engagements.
- **Dual Control Modes:** Seamlessly switch between RTS mode for commanding your squad and a direct-control Top-Down Shooter (TDS) mode for hands-on action.
- **Core Combat:** Experiment with different unit compositions, scale, and time-to-kill.

### The Vision

On each deployment, your small crew enters a fresh battlefield to recover what they can. You're not alone: the local military, wreck survivors, rescue squads, rival scavengers, and bandits are all after the same prize. The longer you stay, the greater the reward, and the danger, creating a press-your-luck loop where greed often meets destruction. Units are persistent across missions, carrying their stats, injuries, and upgrades with them, making every loss meaningful.

### What's Next

For the rest of the year, development will focus on:
- **Improved Encounter Generation:** Randomizing opposing forces based on scenario parameters.
- **Salvaging Implementation:** Introducing proper systems for selling loot, purchasing equipment, and unit customization in the overworld.

### Playtesting

This is a **private playtest**. If you'd like to try the current build and help shape the combat feel of the game, feel free to reach out to me to be added.
